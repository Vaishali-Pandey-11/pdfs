Resources

Purdue OWL: A comprehensive resource for writing, citations (APA, MLA, Chicago), and academic writing tips. Includes resources for both students and instructors.

Google Scholar: A search engine for scholarly articles, journals, and books. Provides access to academic papers and research materials.

Grammarly: An advanced writing assistant for grammar, spelling, and style suggestions. Helpful for writing essays, papers, and assignments.

Evernote: A note-taking tool for organizing study materials, ideas, and resources. Syncs across devices and allows for easy searchability of notes.

RefMe: A citation generator for various citation styles. Helps students create accurate bibliographies and reference lists quickly.
